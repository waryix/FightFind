Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines for safe and productive training sessions.

Pre-Training Safety

  • Always meet in public gyms with other people present
  • Verify partner credentials and experience level
  • Discuss intensity levels and boundaries beforehand
  • Inform someone about your training plans

Training Safety

  • Always use proper protective gear
  • Warm up properly before intense sparring
  • Respect tap-outs and verbal stops immediately
  • Have first aid knowledge and supplies available

Community Standards

  • Treat all community members with respect
  • Report inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Be honest about your skill level and experience
  • Maintain professional communication

Report Issues

  • Use in-app reporting for safety concerns
  • Contact support for technical issues
  • Emergency: Call 911 for medical emergencies
  • Block and report inappropriate users
